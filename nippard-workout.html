NIPPARD PRINCIPLES
📈
Progressive Overload
Add weight or reps each week. The #1 driver of hypertrophy. Track everything.
🎯
10–20 Sets Per Muscle / Week
Stay in the MEV–MRV range. Beginners: 10–12 sets. Advanced: up to 20.
⏱️
Rest 2–3 Min Between Sets
Full recovery = more reps next set = more volume = more gains.
💪
Train 6–30 Rep Range
Most growth happens 6–20 reps. Use heavier loads for compounds, lighter for isolation.
🔁
RIR 0–3 (Near Failure)
Reps In Reserve. Get within 0–3 reps of failure on most sets for max stimulus.
